How to Add Your Photos

To add your own photos to this gallery, simply replace the placeholder emoji icons with your actual images. Place your image files in the same folder as this website and update the gallery items.

Quick Guide:

  1. Save your photos in the website folder (jpg, png formats work best)
  2. Open gallery.html in a text editor
  3. Find the gallery-placeholder divs
  4. Replace them with: <img src="your-photo.jpg" alt="Description" style="width:100%; height:100%; object-fit:cover;">
  5. Save and refresh the page to see your photos!
