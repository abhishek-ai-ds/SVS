About Us

Our Journey of Service and Transformation

Who We Are

Swami Vivekananda Sangha, Kohabarawa is a dedicated non-governmental organization committed to serving humanity through the ideals and teachings of Swami Vivekananda. Founded with a vision to uplift and empower communities, we work tirelessly to bring positive change to the lives of people in Kohabarawa and surrounding areas.

Our organization believes in the inherent strength and potential of every individual. Inspired by Swami Vivekananda's philosophy of service, education, and self-reliance, we strive to create opportunities for holistic development and social transformation.

Our Vision

To create a society where every individual has access to quality education, healthcare, and opportunities for personal and professional growth. We envision communities that are self-reliant, empowered, and united in the spirit of service and compassion.

Our Mission

We are committed to:

  • Providing quality education and literacy programs to underprivileged children and adults
  • Organizing health camps and medical assistance for community members
  • Empowering women through skill development and entrepreneurship programs
  • Nurturing youth leadership and character development
  • Promoting social harmony and cultural values
  • Creating sustainable development initiatives for community welfare

Our Values

Seva (Service)

Selfless service to humanity is at the core of everything we do. We believe that true fulfillment comes from serving others without expectation.

Compassion

We approach every individual with empathy, understanding, and genuine care for their wellbeing and development.

Integrity

We maintain the highest standards of honesty, transparency, and accountability in all our operations and interactions.

Excellence

We are committed to delivering quality in every program and initiative, constantly improving our impact and reach.

Unity

We believe in bringing people together, transcending barriers of caste, creed, and religion to work for the common good.

Empowerment

We focus on building capacity and creating opportunities that enable individuals and communities to become self-reliant.

The Legacy of Swami Vivekananda

Our organization draws deep inspiration from Swami Vivekananda, one of India's greatest spiritual leaders and social reformers. His teachings about the dignity of labor, importance of education, empowerment of women, and service to humanity guide our every action.

Swami Vivekananda believed that education is the manifestation of perfection already in man. Following this principle, we work to unlock the potential within every individual, helping them realize their inherent strength and capabilities.

Our Journey

Since our inception, Swami Vivekananda Sangha has touched the lives of thousands of individuals through various programs and initiatives. What started as a small group of dedicated volunteers has grown into a vibrant organization making a meaningful impact in our community.

We have conducted numerous educational programs, health camps, skill development workshops, and community development initiatives. Each program is designed with care, keeping the needs and aspirations of our beneficiaries at the center.

Our Team

Our strength lies in our dedicated team of volunteers, social workers, educators, and community leaders who bring passion, expertise, and commitment to our mission. Together, we work towards creating lasting positive change in our community.

We are always looking for individuals who share our vision and want to contribute to this noble cause. Whether through volunteering, donations, or partnerships, there are many ways to join hands with us in this journey of service.
