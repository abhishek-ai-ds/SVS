Become a Volunteer

Volunteering with Swami Vivekananda Sangha is a rewarding experience that allows you to make a direct impact on the lives of people in our community. We welcome volunteers from all backgrounds who share our passion for service.

Volunteer Opportunities:

Teaching & Education

Help teach students, assist in tutoring classes, or contribute to our literacy programs.

Health Camps

Support our medical camps through registration, coordination, or health awareness activities.

Skill Training

Share your professional skills to train women and youth in various vocational areas.

Event Organization

Help organize programs, workshops, cultural events, and community gatherings.

Ready to Volunteer?

Fill out the contact form above with "Volunteering" as the subject, and tell us about your interests and availability. We'll get back to you with more information about current opportunities.

Partner With Us

We believe in the power of collaboration. If you represent an organization, business, or institution that shares our vision of community development, we'd love to explore partnership opportunities.

Partnership Areas:

  • Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives
  • Skill development and training programs
  • Health and medical services
  • Educational programs and scholarships
  • Infrastructure development
  • Resource mobilization and fundraising

For partnership inquiries, please contact us using the form above or email us directly at partnerships@svskbw.org

Visit Our Center

You're welcome to visit our community center to learn more about our work, meet our team, and see the impact of our programs firsthand. We recommend scheduling a visit in advance to ensure someone is available to show you around.

To schedule a visit, please use the contact form above or call us during working hours.
